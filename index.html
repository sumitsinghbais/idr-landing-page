About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Through collaboration with academic institutions and industry partners, IDR combines academic insight with real-world projects to prepare the next generation of digital risk professionals.
